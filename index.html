What We Offer

Comprehensive Dental Services

From routine checkups to full smile makeovers — everything your family needs in one welcoming place.

🦷
General & Preventive
Routine cleanings, exams, X-rays, fluoride treatments and sealants — the foundation of lifelong dental health.
✨
Cosmetic Dentistry
Professional whitening, veneers, bonding and complete smile makeovers — designed to give you confidence.
⚙️
Restorative Care
Fillings, crowns, bridges and dentures to fully restore the look, feel, and function of your natural teeth.
👶
Pediatric Dentistry
Child-friendly care from the very first tooth. We make kids feel safe, comfortable and actually excited to visit.
🚨
Emergency Dental
Toothache, broken tooth, knocked-out tooth? We offer same-day emergency slots — call us first.
😴
Sedation Dentistry
For patients with dental anxiety, we offer safe sedation options so you can get the care you need — comfortably.

Our Story

Why Lake Placid Trusts Sunshine

We're more than a dental practice — we're your neighbors. For over 15 years, Sunshine Family Dentistry has been the trusted choice for families across Highlands County.

Our approach is simple: treat every patient the way we'd want our own family treated — with patience, clarity, and genuine warmth.

🤝
Patient-First Philosophy
Treatment plans built around your comfort, goals, and budget — never pushed toward unnecessary procedures.
🔬
Modern Equipment
Digital X-rays, intraoral cameras, and the latest in gentle dental technology for accurate, painless care.

Rooted in the Community
